# Gravity Manipulation and Antigravity Technology Platform

A comprehensive smart contract platform for managing advanced gravitational technologies, including artificial gravity generation, antigravity propulsion, gravitational wave communication, planetary gravity adjustment, and gravity-based energy harvesting.

## System Overview

This platform consists of five interconnected smart contracts that manage different aspects of gravity manipulation technology:

### 1. Gravitational Field Generation Contract (`gravitational-field-generator.clar`)
- Creates and manages artificial gravity fields for space habitats and vehicles
- Controls field strength, direction, and coverage areas
- Monitors field stability and energy consumption
- Manages field activation/deactivation permissions

### 2. Antigravity Propulsion Coordination Contract (`antigravity-propulsion.clar`)
- Coordinates gravity-defying transportation and flight systems
- Manages propulsion unit registration and certification
- Controls flight path authorization and traffic management
- Monitors propulsion system performance and safety

### 3. Gravitational Wave Communication Contract (`gravitational-wave-comm.clar`)
- Utilizes gravity waves for faster-than-light information transmission
- Manages communication channel allocation and encryption
- Controls message routing and delivery confirmation
- Monitors wave interference and signal quality

### 4. Planetary Gravity Adjustment Contract (`planetary-gravity-adjustment.clar`)
- Modifies planetary gravitational fields for terraforming and habitability
- Manages large-scale gravity modification projects
- Controls environmental impact assessments
- Monitors planetary stability and ecosystem effects

### 5. Gravity-Based Energy Harvesting Contract (`gravity-energy-harvesting.clar`)
- Extracts usable energy from gravitational field fluctuations
- Manages energy collection and distribution systems
- Controls harvesting efficiency optimization
- Monitors energy storage and grid integration

## Key Features

- **Decentralized Control**: Each contract operates independently while maintaining system coherence
- **Safety Protocols**: Built-in safety mechanisms and emergency shutdown procedures
- **Energy Management**: Comprehensive energy tracking and optimization
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions for different operational levels
- **Monitoring Systems**: Real-time status tracking and performance metrics

## Technology Stack

- **Smart Contracts**: Clarity language on Stacks blockchain
- **Testing**: Vitest framework for comprehensive test coverage
- **Configuration**: Clarinet for local development and testing

## Contract Interactions

While contracts operate independently, they share common patterns:
- Administrative controls for authorized operators
- Energy consumption tracking and limits
- Safety protocol enforcement
- Status monitoring and reporting
- Emergency shutdown capabilities

## Getting Started

1. Install dependencies: \`npm install\`
2. Run tests: \`npm test\`
3. Deploy contracts using Clarinet: \`clarinet deploy\`
